What We Can Help With

Our team is happy to assist with:

  • Reading order questions for Jujutsu Kaisen manga and the prequel volume
  • Clarification about story arcs like the Shibuya Incident or Culling Game
  • Recommendations for where to find official manga releases
  • General feedback about our website and content
  • Suggestions for new guides or resources you'd like to see

Please note that we focus exclusively on the manga created by Gege Akutami. For questions about adaptations or merchandise, we recommend contacting the official publishers directly.
